Frequently Asked Questions
When and Where is the wedding?
Wedding and Reception is on October 21, 2023 at Johann's Private Resort, Brgy. San Pedro, Sto. Tomas, Batangas
What time should we arrive?
11:00 AM for Groomsmen and Bridesmaids, and 04:00 PM for others in entourage
What should we wear?
We have several dress codes, you may check the dress codes here
Can I bring someone else to your wedding with me?
Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate those who are not part of our guest list due to limited space in our venue.
Do you have a gift preference?
As love is what this day is all about, your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all. If it is your wish to bless us, monetary gift is one we suggest as we will begin our new life together.
Can we take photos or videos during the ceremony?
Our ceremony is unplugged and we aim to keep it solemn. All guests are requested to refrain from taking pictures/videos during the wedding ceremony.
After we announce as husband and wife, you're free to take shots. Most importantly, do not block the view of our hired professional photo & video team. We promised to share the photos after the wedding.
What time will the reception start?
Reception will start after the ceremony, on or before 07:00 PM
Can we sit anywhere during the reception?
NO, it took us a lot of effort and discussion to finish the seating arrangment which is planned for everone's convenience and network preference. So, no need to worry, You'll be surely seated along with your group of friends.
When is the appropriate time to leave?
Hopefully you'll stay with us until the party ends. We aim to end the program at around 09:00 PM
PLEASE DON'T EAT AND RUN :D
